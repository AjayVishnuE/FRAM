HOW FRAM

WORKS?

FRAM is a mobile application to record all farming events, quantities and cost of farming.
FRAM is mobile app that is able to assist the farmers in farming operations by providing timely advisories and alerts based on the selection of crop and other inputs received from the farmer from time to time and guide him through the entire field operations using AI. FRAM acts a guide to new farmers to understand ways of farming.

Educate

Earn basic information and knowledge about farming through FRAM.

Assist

Help farmers to understand about the effective cost and risk in farming.

Update

Provides regular updates based on an effective timeline generated by user.

FEATURES

  • Farm-calculator

    • Provides an overview for financial expenses that will occur in the entire cultivation.

  • Timeline Generator

    • Includes detailed step by step process to be executed during the process of cultivation.

  • Plant disease detection

    • Using ML detect diseases of a crop upon scanning it.

  • Notifications

    • provides in-app notifications along with notifications from KAU and IMD.

  • Beginners guide

    • Provides guidance for beginners in farming. Includes multiple topics regarding farming.
